Hi, I'm David. I enjoy building websites for a living. My expertise is on the presentation layer, where I put my skills to the task of making modern designs come to life.

I approach design by first understanding who will be using the website, what their goals are, and what I can do to make their lives easier. Everything should revolve around the user.

I am an advocate of open source technology and am regularly inspired by people like Mark Otto, Addy Osmani and Luke Wroblewski, who help the modern web move forward.
